Connect to Claude Vision

Internal QuickBooks Online integration · MTAG LLC d/b/a Rackley Consulting

Claude Vision is an internal command-line integration. It has no hosted web interface. To connect a QuickBooks Online company:

  1. Sign in to this machine as an authorized Rackley Consulting user.
  2. From the project directory, run the authorization script:
    python -m scripts.authorize --company <key>
  3. Follow the on-screen prompts to sign in to QuickBooks Online as an admin of the target company and grant access.

If you need help, contact chandler@craincorp.com.
