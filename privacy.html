Privacy Policy

Claude Vision · Last updated April 20, 2026

This policy describes how MTAG LLC, doing business as Rackley Consulting (“Company”) collects and uses data in connection with the internal software application known as Claude Vision (“Software”). The Software is an internal tool used only within Company and its affiliated entities and is not offered to the public.

1. Data We Access

When authorized by an administrator of a QuickBooks Online company owned by Company or an affiliated entity, the Software may read accounting data from that company via the Intuit QuickBooks Online API, including but not limited to accounts, customers, vendors, invoices, journal entries, and financial reports.

2. How We Use Data

Data accessed by the Software is used solely for internal business purposes of Company and its affiliated entities, including financial reporting, reconciliation, and operational analysis. We do not sell, rent, or otherwise disclose this data to third parties for their own use.

3. Storage and Security

Data retrieved by the Software is stored on systems controlled by Company. Access credentials (including OAuth tokens issued by Intuit) are kept in restricted, non-public locations and are accessible only to authorized personnel. Industry-standard administrative, technical, and physical safeguards are used to protect against unauthorized access.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share data accessed by the Software with any party outside Company, except:

  • With Intuit, as an unavoidable consequence of using the QuickBooks Online API (governed by Intuit’s own privacy policy).
  • As strictly required by law, court order, or regulatory authority.

5. Data Retention

Data is retained only as long as reasonably necessary for the internal business purposes described above, or as required by law. Users may request deletion of data accessed by the Software by contacting the address below.

6. Revoking Access

A QuickBooks Online administrator may revoke the Software’s access at any time through Intuit’s “My Apps” console. Once access is revoked, the Software will no longer be able to read data from that company file.

7. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date above.

8. Contact

Questions about this policy may be directed to chandler@craincorp.com.
